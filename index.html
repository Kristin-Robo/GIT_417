The NHL’s 32nd Club

The city of Seattle is now home to the KRAKEN! We welcome 32,000 passionate and eager fans that have awaited this day! Opening day October 12, 2022 is right around the corner and we can’t wait to get this season started.

The 2022-23 campaign will kick off with a two-game road trip that will see the Kraken take on two California teams. The team will then return to Seattle to face expansion-rival Vegas Golden Knights in the team's first home game of the season on Saturday, Oct. 15 at 7 p.m. PT at Climate Pledge Arena.
